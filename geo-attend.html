GeoAttend: GPS-Based Smart Attendance Solution

Simplify and Secure Your Attendance Management

Mark your attendance from your designated work location.

GeoAttend is an innovative attendance management system developed by Znzit, utilizing GPS technology to verify employee presence at designated work locations. It provides a modern, hardware-free alternative to traditional manual timesheets or biometric devices, making it the perfect solution for managing remote field staff, mobile workforces, and hybrid teams. GeoAttend ensures that attendance records are accurate and tied to a verified location, bringing trust and efficiency to your payroll and management processes.

Our Promise: "At GeoAttend, we promise to bring honesty and simplicity to your attendance management."

Transform attendance with GPS verification for your onsite and remote teams.

Frequently asked questions

GeoAttend is a GPS-based smart attendance system that allows employees to mark their attendance from their smartphones, with their location being verified against pre-defined company locations.

Administrators set approved locations (called Points of Interest or POIs). When an employee marks attendance, the app captures the GPS coordinates. The system only approves the punch if it is within the radius of a designated POI.

Yes, employees need an Android or iOS smartphone with location services (GPS) enabled to mark their attendance.

An administrator can log in to the web dashboard, go to the POI section, and add a new Point of Interest by entering the address or dropping a pin on the map and setting a radius.

The system shows the captured coordinates and address for every punch. If there is a dispute, the administrator can review this data to verify the location manually.
