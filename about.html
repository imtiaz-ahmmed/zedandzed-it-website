OUR COMPANY HISTORY

Pioneering IT progress since 2000, turning complex challenges into your success stories.

Zedandzed IT Services Ltd. began in 2000 as part of the Zedandzed Group with a clear mission—to drive innovation in Bangladesh’s ICT sector. Under the leadership of Engr. Munawwar Ahmad, we evolved from product marketing and IT consultancy into a full-scale technology company.

Focused on quality and round-the-clock support, we deliver complete solutions—from custom software and web platforms to enterprise-grade systems. Guided by our motto, Your Vision... Our Solution., we stand as a trusted technology partner for businesses ready to grow and transform.
