LYFE : Live with Your Family Everywhere

Locate, manage, and optimize your team.

Intelligent field force. Real-time location empowerment.

LYFE (Live with your Family Everywhere) is Znzit's visionary Field Force Management solution, designed to bring organizations and their dispersed field teams closer together. By harnessing the power of Location-Based Services (LBS), LYFE creates a seamless, connected, and efficient ecosystem. It provides peace of mind for management and empowers field employees with the tools they need to excel. Our platform integrates cutting-edge GPS tracking, intelligent automation, and robust analytics, transforming raw location data into actionable intelligence that drives productivity, ensures safety, and enhances customer satisfaction.

Our Promise: "Bridging the distance between your field force and your headquarters, so you can focus on growing your business, together."

Empowering your field force with intelligent, real-time location-based insights.

Key Features & Capabilities

The LYFE platform offers a comprehensive suite of tools to manage your mobile workforce effectively:

  • Real-Time GPS Tracking & Live Monitoring
  • Smart Geofencing & Automated Attendance
  • Centralized Task & Activity Management
  • Comprehensive Reporting & Analytics Dashboard
  • Hierarchical Management & Role-Based Access
  • Customer & Location (POI) Management
  • Digital Visit Verification & Proof of Work
  • Route Optimization & Journey Planning

Frequently asked questions

LYFE is a comprehensive GPS-based Field Force Management solution. It uses real-time location intelligence, geofencing, and mobile technology to track field employees, automate tasks like attendance and visit verification, and provide actionable insights to improve productivity and operational efficiency.

The tracking is designed to be efficient and intelligent, capturing location data at configurable intervals. While it does use the phone's GPS, our app is optimized to minimize battery consumption, ensuring it lasts throughout a standard workday.

You can create virtual boundaries (geofences) around specific locations like offices or client sites. When an employee enters or exits these zones, the system automatically records their check-in or check-out time, providing accurate, tamper-proof attendance data.

Absolutely. The platform allows managers to create, assign, and monitor tasks and customer visits in real-time. Field executives receive notifications and can update the status (e.g., pending, in progress, completed) directly from their mobile devices.

Data security is our top priority. We employ robust encryption protocols for all data in transit and at rest. Access to sensitive information is controlled through a strict hierarchical role-based system, ensuring employees only see the data relevant to their role.

LYFE offers a comprehensive reporting suite. You can generate detailed reports on employee attendance, travel history, task completion rates, visit summaries, geofence violations, and location errors, all of which can be exported for further analysis.

The LYFE mobile app can often store data locally when offline. Once the device reconnects to a network, it automatically syncs all the pending data to the central server, ensuring no information is lost.

Visit verification is tied to the employee's real-time GPS location. They cannot manually check in to a visit unless their device's GPS confirms they are within a pre-defined radius of the customer's location, providing reliable proof of presence.
